Meet The Team


Because I am not a one-man show and couldn't be learning all this without their support.

Cora
Position: The Manager
As suggested by the title, manages activities and attempts to coordinate general household operations such as where people should sit, what foods to make, and where items should go. Often willing to provide unsolicited assistance to other household members. Though it must be noted that tasks where assistance is provided have a very low completion rate or tend to take 3x longer when in fact completed. Her skill to avoid lava on the ground is second to none.

Christie
Position: Coordinator of Managerial Demands
Provides all-round care and support for the team. Has magical healing powers when The Manager falls over while avoiding lava pits. Extremely skilled artist who also takes beautiful pictures, though she’ll never send them to you. Christie’s Camera - the place where moments go to be forgotten. Has too many plants.

Eevee
Position: Head of Kitchen Activity
No one shall set foot in the kitchen and go unnoticed. Always willing to police gastronomic activities. Needs to be put in another room during meal times. Swears she will catch that pesky red laser dot one day.
