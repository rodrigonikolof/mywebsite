Hi! I am Rodrigo Nikolof ...


... and where has coding been my whole life?

I honestly feel like a kid who has been given a set of LEGO (a kid who likes LEGO, this metaphor wouldn’t be applicable to a kid who doesn’t) where it is possible to build just about anything.

I look and see that there is so so so much to learn but rather than being overwhelming, it’s enticing and reels me in even further.
